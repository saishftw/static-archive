01 // The Novice Index

Terminology Glossary

Foundational concepts for the logistics software ecosystem.

3PL

Third-Party Logistics. Firms that manage distribution, storage, and fulfillment for other brands.

TMS

Transportation Management System. Software used to plan, execute, and optimize the physical movement of goods.

WMS

Warehouse Management System. Controls and optimizes daily warehouse operations from receiving to shipping.

POD

Proof of Delivery. The document (often paper) confirming goods reached the buyer. A major digital bottleneck.

MSME

Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises. The target size band of this report (₹10–100 Cr revenue).

Tribal Knowledge

Operations based on a dispatcher's memory or gut feeling rather than digital data optimization.

02 // Maturity Audit

The 5-Layer Hypothesis

Verification of the digitization drop-off point across three key regions.

The "Excel Wall"

Layer 2 (Integration) is where adoption collapses. Disconnected silos force manual data re-entry between systems.

Compliance Driven

Layer 1 adoption (45-60%) is largely reactive, driven by GST in India and E-invoicing mandates in KSA.

AI Illusion

Layer 4/5 adoption remains near zero for SMBs, despite marketing claims from enterprise vendors.
